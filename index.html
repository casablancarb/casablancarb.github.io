Who are we?

The Casablanca Ruby Users Group ("Casablanca.rb") is a mostly informal collection of individuals interested in the Ruby programming language.

When do we meet?

Usually, we hold meetups every last Saturday of the month.

How do I join?

Casablanca.rb membership incurs no dues, no fees, and no obligation. Just join the meetup group, show up to the meetings periodically, and you're welcome to call yourself a member of the Casablanca.rb. Its so easy to interact with other professional and hobby Rubyists in your area you'll kick yourself for not doing it sooner!
